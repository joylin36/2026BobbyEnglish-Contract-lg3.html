第一條：就讀班級與班級名稱 / Article 1: Class and Enrollment
1. 本契約就讀班級名稱為： 【Read, Write & Speak with Confidence! Intermediate English Thinking & Sentence Scaffolding Class (Let's Go 3) 聽說讀寫全方位！中級美語思維與句型養成班】。
The enrolled class name is: [Read, Write & Speak with Confidence! Intermediate English Thinking & Sentence Scaffolding Class (Let's Go 3) Let's Go 3 Intermediate English Thinking - Level 3].
第二條：課程科目、內容與授課時數 / Article 2: Course Subjects, Content, and Weekly Hours
1. 課程科目內容：兒童美語進階訓練（涵蓋 Phonics 自然發音進階、生活情境對話、聽說讀寫強化訓練）。
Course Subject Content: Children's English Training (including advanced Phonics, situational dialogues, and intensive training in listening, speaking, reading, and writing).
2. 每週授課時數：每週授課3次，每次2小時，每週共計6小時。詳細上課時間如第三條。
Weekly Hours: 3 times per week, 2 hours per session, totaling 6 hours per week. The detailed class schedule is listed in Article 3 (Attachment 1).
3. 班級編制：採精緻小班制教學，每班實際點名上課人數上限嚴格遵守政府法令規範，以確保教學品質。
Class Size: Exquisite small-class system. The number of students per class shall strictly comply with government regulations to ensure quality.

第四條：修業期間、開課日與認定機制 / Article 4: Course Period, Start Date, and Acknowledgment
1. 修業期間自民國115年09月起至課程結束止。實際正式開課日為民國115年08月31日。
The course period starts from September 2026 (Minguo 115) until the end of the course. The official start date is August 31, 2026.
2. 前項期間或開課日之認定，以甲乙雙方約定（即本契約登載）為準。
The recognition of the aforementioned course period or class start date shall be based on the mutual agreement specified in this contract.
3. 插班認定規範：課程開始後始報名插班者，應以報名後最近一次上課日作為開課日（未填列時以甲方實際第一次上課日為開課日），並以該課程剩餘期間作為總課程時數。
Later Enrollment Clause: For students registering after the course has commenced, the next immediate class date after registration shall be recognized as the start date (if left blank, the student's actual first day of class shall be the start date), and the remaining period of the course shall constitute the total course hours.

This course is a standard systematic progression class and is NOT a "guaranteed success" or "warranty" program (No guarantee contents or period apply).

第七條：桃園市短期補習班退費規定與標準 / Article 7: Refund Policy and Regulations
依據「桃園市短期補習班退費管理自治條例」規定，學員因個人因素離班者，補習班應辦理退費，標準如下：
1. 實際開課日前第30日以前要求退費者：應退還當期開班依約繳納費用之總額。
If requested 30 days or more before the class start date: Full refund of total fees paid.
2. 實際開課日前第1日至第29日要求退費者：應退還當期開班依約繳納費用總額之百分之九十。但所收取金額以新臺幣一千元為限，超過部分仍應退還。
If requested within 1 to 29 days before the start date: 90% refund (the kept amount is capped at NT$1,000).
3. 實際開課日起5日內要求退費者：應退還當期開班依約繳納費用總額之百分之八十。
If requested within 5 days from the start date: 80% refund of the total fees.
4. 實際開課日起之第六日，至全期或總課程時數之三分之一期間內要求退費者：應退還當期開班依約繳納費用總額之百分之五十。
If requested from the 6th day until 1/3 of the total course term: 50% refund of the total fees.
5. 已逾全期或總課程時數之三分之一者：所收取之當期費用，得全數不予退還。
If requested after 1/3 of the total course term: No refund will be provided.
6. 代辦之教材費若已購置且已發放予甲方者，不予退還，教材歸甲方所有。
If purchased materials have already been issued to the student, they are non-refundable and belong to the student.
第八條：請假、可歸責調課與補課機制 / Article 8: Absence, Rescheduling and Make-up Classes
1. 學生若因病、事假無法出席課程，家長應於上課前兩小時透過官方LINE或電話完成請假程序。
Parents must request sick/personal leave via official LINE or phone at least 2 hours prior to the class.
2. 完成請假者，本班將提供該導師安排合適時間進行一對一/小組補課、輔導。
For approved leaves, the school will offer 1-on-1 or small-group make-up classes and tutoring arranged by the instructor at a suitable time.
3. 未依規定請假而無故缺課者，視同放棄該堂課程權益，本班不另行安排個人補課，亦不予扣除學費或退費。
Unexcused absences constitute a waiver of class rights; no individual make-up or refund will be given.
4. 調課補課保障：因可歸責於乙方之事由（如教學人員請假等）調動上課時間，致甲方無法配合而缺課者，乙方應一併予以補課、提供錄影，或經比例計算後返還該節課程費用。
If a class is rescheduled due to reasons attributable to the school and the student cannot attend, the school must provide make-up classes, recordings, or refund the class fee proportionally.
第九條：雙方義務、更換師資與違規終止 / Article 9: Rights, Obligations and Termination
1. 乙方應確保教學場所之公共安全、消防設施及公共意外責任險符合政府法令規範。
Party B shall ensure that public safety, fire facilities, and public liability insurance comply with government laws.
2. 乙方所聘任之任課教師（如：Bobby林仲齊、Joy林婷玉等教學人員）皆應具備合格教學資格，並落實無犯罪紀錄之實名防查登記。無正當事由不得任意更換教學人員，更換時人數不得超過三分之一，且須以相同專業資格者為限。
Teachers must be fully qualified (e.g., Teacher Bobby Lin, Chung-Chi, Teacher Joy Lin, Ting-Yu) with clear criminal records. Instructors shall not be replaced without justification; replacement is capped at 1/3 and restricted to equivalent qualifications.
3. 變更合約終止：乙方無正當事由變更原訂課程、科目、時數、上課地點 or 更換教學人員超過三分之一人數者，甲方得終止契約，乙方應依甲方已繳金額費用加計30%退還甲方。
If Party B alters classes or qualifications by more than 1/3 without cause, Party A may terminate the contract, and Party B must refund the fees paid plus an extra 30% penalty.
4. 甲方於修業期間應遵守課堂秩序，不得有干擾他人學習、破壞公物之行為。若有嚴重危害班級安全、竊奪或違法行為且屢勸不聽者，乙方得終止本契約，並依第七條退費手續辦理。
Party A must maintain order. For major disruptions, thefts, or illegal acts, Party B may terminate the contract and issue refunds according to Article 7.
第十條：肖像權授權與個人資料保護 / Article 10: Portrait Rights and Privacy Protection
1. 甲方同意乙方於課堂教學、節慶活動或成果發表中進行拍照及錄影，並授權乙方得將其影像合理使用於補習班內部學習成果展示、官方網站或招生推廣文宣，且不損及學生名譽。
Party A agrees that Party B may take photos/videos during classes or events for achievement displays or promotions, provided it doesn't harm the student's reputation.
2. 未經甲方書面同意，乙方不得擅自將甲方個人資料提供給第三人或作不當利用。乙方應嚴格遵守《個人資料保護法》之規定，僅用於教學行政與聯絡。
Without written consent, Party B shall not provide personal data to third parties. Data handling must fully comply with the Personal Data Protection Act.
第十一條：疑義處理與契約分存 / Article 11: Interpretation and Miscellaneous
1. 本契約各條款如有疑義時，應依消費者保護法第十一條第二項規定，為有利甲方（消費者）之解釋。
In case of doubts regarding contract clauses, interpretations shall lean in favor of Party A (the consumer) based on the Consumer Protection Act.
2. 本契約一式二份，由甲乙雙方各執一份為憑，並自簽約日起生效。雙方同意合約保存期限不得少於五年。
This contract is executed in duplicate, with each party holding one copy. It takes effect on the date of signing and must be preserved for at least 5 years.
